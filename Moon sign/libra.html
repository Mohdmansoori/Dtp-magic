Libra

Libra, the 7th sign of the Zodiac represents justice, fairness, equality, peace and harmony. Librans are great advocates of human rights and they zealously work to establish equality in everything they do and wherever theygo. They make friends easily and even the most anti-social Libran will give tough competition to other social butterflies across the Zodiac signs. Socializing is an art that's inbuilt in the Libran make-up. They do not need any lessons from anyone when it comes to getting along with others.

Libra is attracted to art and is blessed with a good taste. Elegant and fne surroundings appeal to them and you will find them fussing about, if things are not to their taste. In life, Librans use their head to decide the course of their life and are not driven by emotions. They are highly emotional  and theycare about other people's feelings and needs. It's very tough to lose your cool with them because they always make sense and it seems like they can see right through you. Their logical heads combined with their romantic nature makes them an excellent partner for almost all signs, but it is Aquarius, Gemini, Leo and Sagittarius who are likely to work best with the Libran nature.

Being an air sign, Libra will find it difficult to remain focused on one thing for too long. Their thoughts keep drifting and they are easily distracted by their latest interests. 

There's nothing that doesn't appeal to Libra. They are always ready to experience something new and to give it their all. Commitment and loyalty are traits highly 
valued by a Libran. Since they are never unfair to anyone, they expect people close to them to demonstrate these same values they hold in high regard.
