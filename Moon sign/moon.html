Moon Sign

Universe consists of innumerable stars, planets and heavenly bodies. Stars are grouped into different groups and shapes which can be formed as definitive figures. These groups of stars are called constellations. There are a total of 27 constellations in the sky. All constellations have specific characteristics and these 27 constellations are divided into 12 parts. These 12 parts are known as 12 zodiac signs.

The zodiac from which the moon is passing through at the time of birth is called the moon sign for that individual. This sign establishes the basis for anticipating one’s auspicious or unfavaourable future. In astrology knowledge of the moon sign plays extremely important role in ascertaining quality of life, mind, body, health and success etc. Everybody, from children to old people wish to know what lies in their future. Moon sign is extremely helpful in knowing the future in many things, be it the matters related to money, peace and happiness, travel related matters, or matters related to marriage. Indian astrology system is based on “Niryan Method” in which every zodiac is of 30 degrees. This is the reason there are 12 ascendants in 24 hours and every ascendant is of 30 degrees.

Every moon sign has one lord planet. Lord of Leo and Cancer respectively are the Sun and the Moon. Lord planet of rest of the ten signs is distributed among 5 planets, namely Mercury, Mars, Venus, Jupiter and Saturn.

With lots of efforts, extensive study and research, DTP Magic has developed certain products based solely on characteristics of Lord of each planet which directly affects standards of living, peace, happiness and growth of individuals, namely soap, tilak, mala, oil etc.
